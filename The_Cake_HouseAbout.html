About us:

The Cake House is a artisan bakery that specialises in selling all sorts of breads,pastries and cakes.
The Cake House was founded in 2018 by a man named Joseph Clark who has had a passion for baking since the age of 10.
He grew up in the city of Waterford here in Ireland back in 1964 Where his passion for baking began. Since then he has only perfected his craft for it and wants to share is passion with the world.

We are commited to making sure that our products are baked using the most environmentally friendily produce aswell as making sure that our products are baked to highest standards that we can offer.
One of our core values here at the cake house is our strong relationships that we have with our staff, suppliers and our customers as we believe that by maintaining these relationships that we can truly prosper as not only a business but as a famiily to those that are commited to our services.

Joseph Clark
